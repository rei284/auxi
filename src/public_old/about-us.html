About Auxi

Our Mission

Auxi exists to make learning equitable for everyone. We build an AI study buddy that adapts to different learning styles, supports assistive technologies, and lowers barriers for students of all backgrounds and abilities. Our goal is simple: deliver inclusive, accessible tools that help every learner understand, practice, and grow—at their own pace and on their own terms.

We believe that technology should amplify potential—not privilege. By centering accessibility and community in our design process, Auxi empowers students with clear explanations, personalized study plans, and supportive feedback that cultivates confidence and curiosity.
