About Auxi

Auxi exists to make learning equitable for everyone. Our AI study buddy is designed to support students with ADHD, dyslexia, and other learning differences by adapting to individual learning styles and integrating with assistive technologies. We prioritize inclusivity and accessibility, ensuring that no student faces discrimination based on ability.

We believe that technology should amplify potential—not privilege. Our goal is simple: provide tools that help every learner understand, practice, and grow at their own pace.
