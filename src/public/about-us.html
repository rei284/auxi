Our Mission: Amplify Every Voice in the Classroom.

We build technology that removes barriers, celebrates neurodiversity, and makes exceptional learning accessible to all.

The Auxi Philosophy

Dyslexia Support

Includes text-to-speech functionality, dyslexia-friendly font options, and reduced visual clutter to significantly aid reading and comprehension.

ADHD Focus Assistance

Breaks down complex material into micro-learning modules, employs text chunking, and provides interactive summaries to maintain focus and prevent cognitive overload.

Global Accessibility

Guarantees full compatibility with standard screen readers and enhanced keyboard navigation, ensuring access regardless of the user's assistive technology.
